About SkillSync AI

A smart productivity and study management platform designed to help students stay organized, focused, and consistent.

Why This Project?

Many students struggle with productivity, consistency, task management, and maintaining organized study notes.

SkillSync AI was created to provide students with a centralized platform where they can manage study tasks, save notes, track productivity, and receive AI-powered study suggestions.

The goal of this project is to make studying more structured, efficient, and motivating.

Technologies Used

  • Python
  • Flask
  • SQLite
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Bootstrap
  • JavaScript
  • CS50 SQL Library
